Books

Field Experiments: Design, Analysis, and Interpretation

Alan S. Gerber (Yale University) & Donald P. Green (Columbia University) · W.W. Norton

The definitive guide to experimental design in the social sciences. Covers randomization, estimation, and the practical challenges of running experiments in real-world settings.

All of Statistics: A Concise Course in Statistical Inference

Larry Wasserman · Springer, 2010

A compact but rigorous overview of statistical theory. Covers probability, inference, and machine learning in one volume. Dense but rewarding.
